关于爱红酒(iWine)

iWine.com
爱红酒网站（www.iWine.com)源于我们对葡萄酒的热爱。
作为一家科技公司，我们相信信息的价值在于分享；同时，作为葡萄酒爱好者，我们相信葡萄酒的真正乐趣也在于分享。用信息化的手段，帮助提升葡萄酒的认知和分享，是爱红酒网站的出发点。
我们努力了解普通的葡萄酒消费者的需求，开发简洁易用的工具和界面，并尝试推动“葡萄酒是大众的葡萄酒”的概念。我们并且重视资深葡萄酒爱好者和业内人士的需求，为其度身定制特定的工具和模块，帮助其更好的记录、发布、传播有价值的葡萄酒内容与资讯。
我们让Patrick项目横跨多个平台，让查询、记录、分享云端化。
而这一切，都完全免费！

一切只为更简单、更美好的葡萄酒的信息分享。
来，加入“爱红酒”的葡萄酒世界吧！

iWine.com derives from our deep love towards wine.
As an InfoTech company, we have faith in the value of sharing, and the same faith applies when we are wine lovers. We try to make the sharing of wine information, knowledge and enjoyment easier through infotech means, which is the fundamental of the iWine website and its affiliates.
We’re studying hard to understand what the wine consumers need on vast web platforms, and hence developing the simply and clean UIs to promote our belief of “wine is people’s wine.” We are also focusing on the needs of the wine snobs and professionals, and developed some tailor-made tools and functions for better recording, sharing and broadcasting those contents and knowledge about wine.
We make iWine compatible through multiple platforms, and on the Cloud.
All these are FREE!
For a single purpose to make a better shared world of wine and enjoyment.
Come and join us in iWine!

参与爱红酒

“爱红酒”没有编辑部，网站呈现所有原汁原味的内容；没有写手，所有用户都是写手；没有固定的排名，你的“赞”、“喜欢”、“推荐”就是在为内容排名！ 我们欢迎您加入“爱红酒”的网站，跟亿万网民一起，为红酒、为享受美好的生活一起干杯！

We have no editors but display the original content, no writer in house but every user is the writer, no ranks but whenever user puts a “good”, “like”, “recommend” it contributes to the ranking. We sincerely welcome your joining iWine, and wish you a nice stay here with millions of other wine lovers for a better enjoyment of wine.
